Investment thesis

Thesis

What I believe about the future, and where I look for companies.

Biology is becoming an engineering discipline

The convergence of synthetic biology, computational tools, and AI is turning biology from a discovery science into a design science. Companies that treat biological systems as programmable will build the most durable moats.

AI needs biology as much as biology needs AI

The richest data in the world is biological. Protein sequences, cellular interactions, clinical outcomes — these are the training sets for the next generation of foundation models. The companies that own this data flywheel win.

India's deeptech moment

India has world-class biology talent, growing compute infrastructure, and a regulatory environment that's learning fast. The arbitrage between talent cost and problem value is enormous at pre-seed and seed.

Literature

Poems. Some finished, some still breathing.

  • The lab at night

    The centrifuge hums its lullaby,
    tubes spinning their small deaths,
    separating what was whole
    into what we can measure.

    I am learning the grammar of cells —
    how they speak in phosphorylation,
    argue through apoptosis,
    forgive through division.

  • Chennai, 6 AM

    The city wakes in layers:
    first the crows, then the temples,
    then the autorickshaws
    negotiating with god and traffic.

    I walk to work through the heat
    that arrives before the sun,
    carrying equations in my head
    and my grandfather's voice in my chest.

  • On reading Taleb at twenty-three

    He said: the fragile breaks,
    the robust endures,
    but the antifragile — it feeds on chaos.

    I closed the book and looked
    at my failed startup,
    my rejected papers,
    my unanswered emails,

    and thought: maybe I am not broken.
    Maybe I am composting.
